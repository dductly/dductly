# dductly

A simple, secure tool to track your business expenses and income year-round. Stop scrambling through receipts at tax time - log your expenses and income as they happen and generate clean reports when you need them.

## Features

- Track expenses and income
- Upload receipts and attachments
- View statistics and insights
- Import data from CSV files
- Dark mode support
- Works on desktop and mobile

## User Guide

### Getting Started

1. **Sign Up** - Create an account with your email and business name
2. **Sign In** - Log in to access your dashboard
3. **Dashboard** - View your total expenses, income, and profit at a glance

### Adding Expenses

1. Go to **Expenses** from the dashboard
2. Click **+ Add Expense**
3. Fill in the date, amount, category, vendor, and payment method
4. Optionally upload a receipt or photo by dragging a file or clicking the upload area
5. Click **Add Expense** - you'll be taken to your expenses list

### Adding Income

1. Go to **Income** from the dashboard
2. Click **+ Add Income**
3. Fill in the date, amount, category, customer, and payment method
4. Add a tip amount if applicable
5. Optionally upload supporting documents
6. Click **Add Income** - you'll be taken to your income list

### Viewing & Editing Entries

- Click the **three dots** menu on any expense or income entry
- Select **View** to see all details and attachments
- Select **Edit** to modify the entry or add/remove attachments
- Select **Delete** to remove the entry

### Uploading Attachments

- Supported on both desktop and mobile
- Drag and drop files or click to browse
- Upload photos, PDFs, receipts, or any file type
- View attachments anytime from the View or Edit modal

### Statistics

- View charts and breakdowns of your expenses and income
- Filter by date range and category
- See your profit over time

### Importing Data

- Go to **Import Data** to upload a CSV file
- Map your columns to the correct fields
- Import multiple entries at once

### Settings

- Toggle dark mode for comfortable viewing
- Auto-logout after 15 minutes of inactivity keeps your data secure

## Tips

- Upload receipts right when you make a purchase so you don't lose them
- Use categories consistently to get accurate statistics
- Check your Statistics page regularly to track your profit
